On-Site Power Substation

You can ensure that your data will always be available and without any hiccups. Being adjacent to a power substation allows for greater power capacity. This our testament to our 100% SLA uptime guarantee.

Twelve-Inch Thick Walls

Built specifically as a data center, the 12-inch thick concrete walls establish this as a hardened, weather-resistant facility, capable of withstanding high winds and other potential threats

Concrete Roof

Much sturdier and structurally sound than the typical rubber roof, our concrete roof can protect against any inclement weather, including hail storms or tornadoes.

Thirty-Six-Inch Recessed Floors

Having the data center equipment raised 3 feet allows for greater distribution of cold air below the perforated floors. They also allow for easier to install and maintain under-floor circuitry and conduits for cabling without restricting airflow.

Security Aisles

Utilizing security aisles keeps infrastructure like computer room air conditioners and power distributions units out of the data room and reduces the amount of human traffic inside the facility while keeping our customer’s systems safe and secure.

18MW Total Power Capacity

Access to enough power for today’s clients and for two additional data center expansions which will result in a facility with over 1,700 colocation cabinets.

Sixth Generation Cloud Hub

The new Dublin data center will also house one of our 6th Generation Cloud Hubs, which has been recognized for the second year in a row, by Cloud Spectator, to provide the best performance/value mix in the industry besting providers like Amazon and Azure by more than 10 times the performance in multiple categories.k

More Than a Dozen Fiber Providers

Having access to numerous fiber options makes it easier for our clients to connect to the data center and being carrier neutral gives our customers the ability to choose who is the best provider for them.

Interconnected to Expedient’s 10 other data centers The new Dublin facility is connected via multiple 10Gbps circuits to all of the Expedient’s 10 other data centers to enable easy data replication and provide numerous solutions to achieve true high availability.

Since arriving in Columbus, Expedient has been fortunate to complete two data center builds in Upper Arlington alone, largely thanks to the lasting relationship we’ve built with the technology community. The loyalty and partnerships we’ve built with our customers have brought us to where we are today, and allowed us to move forward with building a brand new facility in Dublin.
